I'm Andy — a drummer, guitarist and composer based in the UK.

My job, whichever instrument I'm holding, is the same: **serve the song**. Behind the kit that means feel and dynamics before flash. On guitar it means texture and taste. As a composer it means knowing when to leave space.

I've spent two decades doing this professionally — touring, tracking sessions, and more recently scoring for screen. I've played sold-out rooms and silent studios, and I care about both equally.

If you're planning a show, a session, or a score, I'd love to hear about it. I promise to take your music as seriously as you do — and to have a laugh while we're at it.
