After eighteen months of late nights, **Nightshift** is finally here.

## Where it came from

The record began as a way to stay creative between session work — a series of after-hours improvisations that slowly turned into songs. I wanted something that felt like a city after midnight.

## How to listen

Stream it on your platform of choice from the [Music page](/music/), or grab a lossless copy on Bandcamp. If you catch a show this year, you'll hear most of it live.
